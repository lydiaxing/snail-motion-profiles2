General directions for use and FAQ

Why is it called "snail"/"Acha"/"Achatinidae"?

It's not because it's slow! My nickname on my robotics team is snail, so I use it for naming a lot.

How do I use this?

Add waypoints using the Waypoint Dashboard - these new waypoints will show up on the "Preview" canvas so you can get a sense of what your trajectory will look like. You can reset the canvas if you delete or edit waypoints. Then, just click generate! A text file with the JSON of your path points will be downloaded to your computer.
